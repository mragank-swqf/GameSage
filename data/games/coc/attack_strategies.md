# Attack Strategies

### Guidelines

#### Basics

- Attack Strategies should only be about army compositions.
- All Attack Strategies should be in the "Attack Strategies" namespace.

#### Categories

- All Attack Strategies should be in the "Attack Strategies" category. This is done automatically if you create your Attack Strategy using the template that is preloaded below.
- Add the category " Your Username 's Attack Strategies" to your Attack Strategies.
- Add the {{Maintenance}} to your Attack Strategy while it is under development.

- This is done automatically if you create your Attack Strategy using the template that is preloaded below.

### Create a Guide

To create a new Attack Strategy, enter the name of your strategy (it must be unique, no slashes ['/'], hashes ['#'] or colons [':']) and then click on 'Add New Strategy' below!

Please do not make Attack Strategies with little or no info on it or it will be deleted by a Staff Member .
