# Honor System

Honor is the system that serves to measure the social acceptability of the protagonists' actions in Red Dead Redemption , Red Dead Redemption 2 , and Red Dead Online . Specific actions in the game are considered honorable or dishonorable.

### Description

#### Red Dead Redemption

Players earn honor by performing positive actions, like saving citizens from danger or aiding lawmen. Alternatively, players lose honor by taking negative actions, like killing lawmen, murdering innocents, aiding outlaws, and stealing. If authorities are made aware of players' negative actions (usually when witnessed by lawmen or eyewitnesses ), Marston will also incur a bounty . Honor will affect how NPCs interact with Marston, depending on whether he has accumulated high or low honor. The psychological effect of honor on NPCs is relative to Marston's fame level.

Having neutral honor has no effects, whereas high and low honor have their own benefits (and penalties). If Marston descends to rank -3, he can beckon a unique horse , as long as his honor doesn't rise to neutral. However, if Marston has high honor, he is bestowed a unique outfit , and if his honor reaches rank 3, he will eventually to encounter a nun who bestows the Obscuridad del Santo Andres , which makes enemies less accurate. If maximum honor is maintained after receiving the item, random nuns will occasionally gift Marston with money or ask for charity (~$70).

When wearing a bandana , actions that would normally cause a change in Marston's honor, whether positive or negative, have no effect. It also prevents any increase in Marston's fame, so it is advisable that players remove it before the end of a mission .

These are the honor ranks in order, from highest to lowest:

- 3 (Hero): Lawmen and civilians only report crimes after first murder or bank robbery . A nun bestows a unique kit item.
- 2 (Peacemaker): Shop prices are improved by 50%, except in Thieves' Landing . The Duster Coat is unlocked.
- 1 (Honest Joe): Jobs , including Bounty Hunting , pay double.
- 0 (Drifter): No effect.
- -1 (Rustler): The cost to bribe eyewitnesses is reduced by 50%.
- -2 (Road Agent): Shop prices are improved by 50% in Thieves' Landing. Marston insults people instead of greeting them.
- -3 (Desperado): Civilians only report crimes after the first murder or bank robbery. Whistling summons the Dark Horse .

#### Red Dead Redemption 2

Honor is a returning system in Red Dead Redemption 2 . Unlike its predecessor, honor plays a more important role in the story. Arthur Morgan 's honor is capped at the beginning of the game and can't be maxed out as fully bad or good until after " A Fork in the Road ".

Like in Redemption , the player can receive discounts from various stores if they have high honor. Stores offer a 10% discount at rank 2, a 25% discount at rank 4, and a 50% discount at ranks 7-8. Meanwhile, having low honor allows the player to loot better-quality items from dead bodies. Honor is scored as an internal value in the game, ranging from -320 to +320.

Upon reaching rank 4, the Cattleman 's "Hero's Pearl Grip" becomes available. Upon reaching rank -4, the Double-Action 's "Gunslinger Ebony Grip" becomes available. The latter can sometimes be acquirable with rank 4 honor, likely due to a glitch.

Arthur's honor affects dialogue in many of the game's cutscenes, as well as the story's main ending. A high-honor Arthur is more selfless and considerate of others, putting a greater emphasis on keeping the gang safe and helping John's family.

Contrarily, a low-honor Arthur is depicted as a more selfish person, prioritizing his own desires over those of others, also being proud and more motivated by money, displaying qualities of greediness and vengefulness while being apathetic to the struggles of others.

In his visions, Arthur sees a buck basking in the sun's rays (high honor) or a black coyote in the rain (low honor). During the voice flashbacks in both his last ride and after his tuberculosis diagnosis, the words Arthur remembers are more positive and hopeful (high honor) or negative and defeatist (low honor).

Arthur's death is ultimately determined by his honor: he either succumbs to his illness and battle wounds (high honor), or is killed directly by Micah Bell (low honor). The Wittemore outfit either has a blue (high honor) or red (low honor) waistcoat and tie.

Some cutscenes are altered depending on Arthur's honor.

- After Dutch kills Gloria in Guarma , a high-honor Arthur will tell him he keeps "killing folk", but a low-honor Arthur simply says that they "have all gotten rotten".
- In " That's Murfree Country ", Arthur's response in the intro cutscene differs. "I guess I'm more interested in surviving (low) / savin' lives (high) than... winnin' at chess."
- In " Money Lending and Other Sins VI & VII ", when the attack on J. John Weathers begins, Arthur will say "I may as well protect the payment" (low) or "I'll do what I can for ya" (high). Later, when Arthur evicts Leopold Strauss , their dialogue will differ.
- In " The Bridge to Nowhere ", Arthur's conversations with John are altered.
- In " The Fine Art of Conversation ", after seeing Lyndon Monroe onto the train, Arthur will see Orville Swanson waiting. Their conversation differs depending on Arthur's honor. Moreover, if the player has completed " Of Men and Angels " and has high honor, Sister Calderón appears instead.
- In " Mrs. Sadie Adler, Widow Part II", after killing Tom , Sadie tells Arthur "Ya know... You're a confusin' man, but... you might just be the best friend I got, Arthur." (low) or "Aside from my Jake , you're the best man I've known." (high).
- In " Our Best Selves ", Arthur tells Dutch "You gotta do what's right and cut some loose. John and his family." (low) or "Y'know, the women and the children... a-and John a-and his family." (high).

Arthur's level of honor also affects his legacy. With high honor, his tombstone is pristine and adorned with flowers, with his epitaph reading " Blessed Are Those Who Hunger And Thirst For Righteousness ". An eagle also spawns near the tombstone. With low honor, Arthur's tombstone is weathered, reading " Blessed Are Those Who Mourn For They Will Be Comforted ". Also, a coyote spawns.

Charles Smith 's dialogue is affected, depending on whether Arthur died with high or low honor.

- In " Bare Knuckle Friendships ", Charles says "He was a good man... As much as any of us could be..." (high honor) or "He wasn't a good man... None of us were, but, uh... Maybe he was true to himself" (low honor).
- In " American Venom ", when pursuing Micah, Charles either claims that Arthur had lost interest in getting revenge towards the end and that he wouldn’t have cared (high honor), or that Arthur would have wanted revenge (low honor).

Unlike with Arthur, John Marston 's honor does not affect his personality in any way, nor does it influence the events of the story. However, gameplay changes, such as store discounts, still apply to John.

There is no way to stop honor from changing in Red Dead Redemption 2 . Unlike its predecessor, wearing a bandana does not grant the player amnesty from changes in honor: it only prevents eyewitnesses from immediately recognizing the player. This is because, in this game, honor is more about Arthur's self-reflection instead of his reputation. Players should also note that, in Chapter 6 , an action that results in a change of honor will have a multiplier of ×1.5 on the honor bar.

#### Red Dead Online

Honor in Red Dead Online functions very similarly to Red Dead Redemption 2 . Players start with neutral honor, which can be increased or decreased through their actions. Committing crimes in Free Roam such as murdering NPCs or other players, antagonizing people, and stealing horses will result in a minor honor loss. An honor increase can be obtained by various means, such as bonding with your horse, throwing fish back into the water after catching them, and sparing gang leaders after clearing gang hideouts . Players can visit Old Man Jones at any time to reset their honor level for the price of 3 gold bars.

Major honor gains/losses occur during the story missions . The missions " Love and Honor ", " The Right Side of the Tracks ", and " If the Hat Fits... " feature a moral choice that will significantly increase or decrease the player's honor level depending on the choice they made. These missions can be replayed at any time, allowing players to maximize/minimize their honor level in a quick fashion.

Doing missions for Strangers will also have a major affect on the player's honor level. There are different types of stranger missions, which will either earn the player honor (e.g. Caravan Escorts, Rescues, Deliveries etc.), or cause them to lose some (e.g. Paid Killings, Horse Thefts, Coach Holdups etc.). Before accepting a stranger mission, the player will be informed of its type and whether it will result in an honor gain or loss.

Unlike Red Dead Redemption 2 , high honor does not provide store discounts and plays a significant role only in the story missions. The player will be presented with one of two paths depending on their honor level: the Path of the Gunslinger (High Honor) or the Path of the Outlaw (Low Honor). Each path features four different missions, but doesn't affect the overall narrative, as the final mission will be the same regardless of the chosen path.

### Trivia

#### Red Dead Redemption

- Neither honor nor fame is used in the Undead Nightmare DLC pack.
- Some characters, like Trent Oxley and Wilton Glover , only appear as dueling challengers when the player has low honor. Their reasons for challenging the player are generally negative, such as getting revenge on the player for killing a family member of theirs.
- The easiest places to lose honor are Thieves Landing and Casa Madrugada , since no bounty is incurred for crimes committed there.
- The fastest way to lose honor is by attacking lawmen, bribing them, re-attacking them and bribing them again, though it is difficult to pull off.
- There is a known glitch in which to earn extra honor. After having accepted a bounty, the game must be saved and the bounty returned alive for an increase in honor. Then, without saving, the player must kill themselves; the game will reload to before the bounty was claimed, but will still have the gain in honor and money and may claim the bounty again. This does not always work, and may only work once per bounty.

#### Red Dead Redemption 2

- In some cases, repeatedly committing an act which normally may result in a small loss of honor (such as not skinning an animal after killing it) may result in a major loss of honor.
- The player will not lose honor when antagonizing Micah in the Van der Linde gang camp. In addition, the gang will not chastise Arthur for antagonizing Micah, and will not get knocked out by John or Lenny.
- If the player kills members of the Ku Klux Klan , they will gain honor rather than losing it. Additionally, if the player kills Jeremiah Compson , a former slave-catcher, in " The Iniquities of History ," they will gain honor as well.
