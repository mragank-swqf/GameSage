# Trophies Guide

Trophies indicate a player's or Clan 's skill rating. Players can gain or lose Trophies by either winning or losing battles . Earning Trophies unlocks rewards via different Trophy Roads, and sometimes unlocks new Arenas and cards.

### Trophy Road Trophies

This type of trophy is the standard one used in the Trophy Road. Gaining more of them allows the player to advance through the Arenas and unlock more cards, improve their chests, and allow them to request or donate more cards from their clans. From 10,000 to 15,000 , the number of trophies win/lost increases to around 150 (instead of around 30).

- Arena 1, Goblin Stadium, is unlocked upon completing the tutorial .
- 300 unlocks Arena 2, Bone Pit.
- 600 unlocks Arena 3, Barbarian Bowl.
- 1,000 unlocks Arena 4, Spell Valley.
- 1,300 unlocks Arena 5, Builder's Workshop.
- 1,600 unlocks Arena 6, P.E.K.K.A's Playhouse.
- 2,000 unlocks Arena 7, Royal Arena.
- 2,300 unlocks Arena 8, Frozen Peak.
- 2,600 unlocks Arena 9, Jungle Arena.
- 3,000 unlocks Arena 10, Hog Mountain.
- 3,400 unlocks Arena 11, Electro Valley.
- 3,800 unlocks Arena 12, Spooky Town
- 4,200 unlocks Arena 13, Rascal's Hideout.
- 4,600 unlocks Arena 14, Serenity Peak.
- 5,000 unlocks Arena 15, Miner's Mine.
- 5,500 unlocks Arena 16, Executioner's Kitchen.
- 6,000 unlocks Arena 17, Royal Crypt.
- 6,500 unlocks Arena 18, Silent Sanctuary.
- 7,000 unlocks Arena 19, Dragon Spa.
- 7,500 unlocks Arena 20, Boot Camp.
- 8,000 unlocks Arena 21, Clash Fest.
- 8,500 unlocks Arena 22, PANCAKES!.
- 9,000 unlocks Arena 23, Valkalla.

- 10,000 unlocks Seasonal 1.
- 11,000 unlocks Seasonal 2.

- 13,500 unlocks Seasonal 4.
- 15,000 unlocks Seasonal 5.

Once a player unlocks an Arena, the player cannot be demoted to a lower one, which means the player’s Trophies will not drop below the required amount of Trophies for that Arena. Players over 10,000 will get reset to between 10,000 and 12,000 at the season reset on the first Monday of each month.

### Starsteel

This type of trophy is the one used in Merge Tactics. Gaining more of them allows the player to advance through Merge Tactics Leagues.

- 0 unlocks Bronze 1.
- 200 unlocks Bronze 2.
- 400 unlocks Bronze 3.
- 700 unlocks Silver 1.
- 1,000 unlocks Silver 2.
- 1,300 unlocks Silver 3.
- 1,700 unlocks Gold 1.
- 2,100 unlocks Gold 2.
- 2,500 unlocks Gold 3.
- 3,000 unlocks Diamond 1.

Once a player unlocks a League, the player cannot be demoted to a lower one, which means the player’s Starsteels will not drop below the required amount of Starsteels for that League.

### Tournament Trophies

- This section describes content that has been replaced or removed from the game. This information is kept here for historical purposes.

- This type of trophy formerly appeared only in Custom Tournaments. It was used in a similar fashion to regular Trophies, but could only be gained or lost within Custom Tournaments.
- At the end of the Custom Tournament, players with more Tournament Trophies would be rewarded with bigger Tournament Chests .

### Legend Trophies

- This section describes content that has been replaced or removed from the game. This information is kept here for historical purposes.

Legend Trophies could be obtained above 3000 at the end of every season. Any Trophies above 3000 would be converted to Legend Trophies and added to the Player's Profile . Legend Trophies added up after each season. This system was replaced by Leagues in the March 2017 Update . However, any players who may have achieved Legend Trophies before had the amount they achieved shown in their profile, until the December 2018 Update , which removed them in exchange for an exclusive Emote .

### Leagues

- This section describes content that has been replaced or removed from the game. This information is kept here for historical purposes.

- Leagues were a special feature that unlocked upon reaching 5,000 .
- The season reset on the first Monday of each month.
- At the end of each season, players in a League lost a certain percentage of their Trophies above 5,000. The percentage depends on what League the player finished in. In the Challenger Leagues, the reset percentage was 50%. In the Master Leagues, the reset percentage was 40%. In the Champion Leagues, the reset percentage was 30%.
- Unlike Arenas, players could drop from a higher League to a lower one.
- Leagues were removed in the in favor of the Ranked system.

- In the Challenger Leagues, the reset percentage was 50%.
- In the Master Leagues, the reset percentage was 40%.
- In the Champion Leagues, the reset percentage was 30%.

### History

2016

- Trophies were added with Starsteel on 4/1/2016.
- On 2/2/2016 the February 2016 Update added the Legendary Arena, which unlocked at 3,000 , as well as Legend Trophies.
- On 29/2/2016 the March 2016 Update added a new Arena , Builder's Workshop, which unlocked at 1,700 , and pushed the lower limit of Royal Arena to 2,000 .
- On 11/4/2016, a maintenance break fixed an issue where some players would not recieve Legendary Trophies after the season reset.
- On 10/5/2016, a maintenance break fixed an issue where some players would not get reset back to 3,000 after the season reset.
- On 23/5/2016, a maintenance break fixed the issue with end of season trophies of some players.
- On 31/5/2016, a maintenance break fixed the issue with end of season trophies.
- On 3/6/2016, a maintenance break fixed the issue with end of season trophies of some players.
- On 4/7/2016, the Tournaments Update added a new Arena, Frozen Peak, which unlocked at 2,300 .
- On 15/8/2016, a Minor Update increased the Trophy infusion level to 2,000 from 1,000. This meant that up to 2,000 , the winner would gain more trophies than the loser lost. Above this point, the Trophies won by the winner would be equal to the Trophies lost by the loser.
- On 29/8/2016, a Minor Update increased the Seasonal Trophy reset from 3,000 to 4,000, in an effort to decrease the number of mismatches, where for example a Level 9 player is matched against a Level 13.

2017

- On 13/1/2017, a new Arena, the Jungle Arena, was added, which unlocked at 2,600 .
- On 13/3/2017, the March 2017 Update added a new Legendary Arena at 3,800 and renamed the old Legendary Arena to Hog Mountain. Leagues were also added to the game, starting from 4,000 and ending at 6,400 . The Trophy reset was also changed so that every players would depend on their League instead of being set to 4,000 for all players. It also made Legendary Trophies no longer accumulate.
- On 12/12/2017, the Electrifying Update added a new Arena, Electro Valley, which unlocked at 3,400 .

2018

- On 20/6/2018, the Summer 2018 Update changed the reset system of the Leagues with each player's reset point now dependent on the player's Trophies rather than their Leagues.
- On 5/12/2018, the December 2018 Update removed the Legend Trophies from players who had them, but to compensate, players who had them received Gold and an exclusive Royal Ghost Emote .

2019

- On 28/1/2019, the January 2019 Update added a new Arena, Spooky Town, which unlocked at 3,600 . It also changed the Trophy requirement for some Arenas.
- On 15/4/2019, the April 2019 Update added the Royal Champion League at 6,700 , and moved the Ultimate Champion Trophy requirement to 7,000 . It also changed the reset system of the Leagues with each player's reset point now being 25% of their Trophies above 4,000 instead of 50%.
- On 6/5/2019, a Balance Update reverted the previous change, making each player's reset point now being 50% of their Trophies above 4,000 instead of 25%. The Trophy Road rewards above 4,000 were also slightly changed.
- Starting from 1/7/2019 with the July 2019 Update , Trophy Road rewards would change depending on the Season.
- On 1/8/2019, a Minor Update fixed an issue for Trophy Road progression indicator sometimes showing the incorrect value.
- On 30/9/2019, the End of September 2019 Update fixed a bug where players would experience a Season Trophy reset when they first reached the Trophy Leagues. It also fixed an issue where the UI did not refresh correctly on the Pass reward track after closing the Trophy Road.

2020

- On 28/2/2020, a maintenance break changed the reset system of the Leagues with each player's reset point now being 75% of their Trophies above 4,000 instead of 50%. This change was reverted on 6/4/2020, in a Balance Update .
- On 4/12/2020, a maintenance break increased the amount of Trophies that a player would lose after losing a battle .

2021

- On 7/1/2021, a maintenance break decreased the amount of Trophies that a player would lose after losing a battle at 4,000 to 6,000 .
- On 1/2/2021, the February 2021 Update changed the reset system of the Leagues with each player's reset point now being 75% of their Trophies above 4,000 instead of 50%. This change was reverted on 1/3/2021, in the March 2021 Update .
- On 25/2/2021, a maintenance break changed the matchmaking system by considering King Level into the equation, prioritizing matches within 1 Level of King Level as opponents for players.
- On 17/3/2021, a maintenance break made matchmaking changes introduced on 25/2/2021 no longer apply above 5,000 .
- On 7/6/2021, the Summer 2021 Update added 2 new Arenas: Rascal's Hideout, from Season 10, which unlocked at 4,200 , and Serenity Peak, from Season 7, which unlocked at 4,600 . The trophy requirement for each League was increased by 1,000, and the Trophy Road was expanded accordingly. The seasonal Trophy reset mechanics for players in Leagues were also updated, and the amount of trophies that a player would lose in the Challenger leagues was decreased.

2022

- On 26/10/2022, the 2022 Quarter 3 Update added 5 new Arenas: Miner's Mine, from Season 34, which unlocked at 5,000 , Executioner's Kitchen, from Season 27, which unlocked at 5,500 , Royal Crypt, from Season 17, which unlocked at 6,000 , Silent Sanctuary, a new Arena, which unlocked at 6,500 , and Dragon Spa, from Season 11, which unlocked at 7,000 . The update also removed Leagues, moving them to a separate Ranked system that was introduced in the same update.
- On 22/12/2022, a maintenance break made some matchmaking improvements.

2023

- On 19/6/2023, the Card Evolution Update added 3 new Arenas: Boot Camp, from Season 46, which unlocked at 7,500 , Clash Fest, from Season 39, which unlocked at 8,000 , and PANCAKES!, from Season 37, which unlocked at 8,500 .
- On 30/6/2023, a maintenance break fixed an issue where Trophy Road rewards were resetting for some players.

2024

- On 30/7/2024, the Goblin Queen's Journey Changes Update added a new Arena, Party Beach, which unlocked at 3,000 .
- On 30/10/2024, the Removal of Goblin Queen's Journey Update removed Goblin Trophies.
- Starting 4/11/2024, the November 2024 , added 2v2 League Trophies.

2025

- Starting 6/05/2025, the May 2025 , added Undead March Trophies.
- On 30/6/2025, the Quarter 2 Update added Starsteel. This also added 6 new Arenas: Valkalla, from Season 55, which unlocked at 9,000 , and 5 Seasonal Arenas that unlocked at 10,000, 11,000, 12,000, 13,500, and 15,000
