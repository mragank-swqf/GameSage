# Arenas Progression

The Trophy Road is a road in Clash Royale that is unlocked after completing the Tutorial . It allows players to compete against each other as the player's Trophy count increases. These battles take part in Arenas that will unlock new rewards, like Lucky Chests , and players with a higher Arena will contain more Gold and Cards . There are a total of 32 Arenas that span within 14,000 . Once a player advances to a higher Arena, they cannot be demoted to a lower Arena.

### Unlockables

- Every time a player advances to a new Arena, Chests and Lucky Chests are improved, the Victory Gold and Gold Rush Gold increases, and Card Donation limit increases.
- Goblin Stadium (Arena 1) unlocks TV Royale , Merge Tactics and the Shop .
- Bone Pit (Arena 2) unlocks Magic Items , Clan Donation and Pass Royale . It also allows Lucky Chests to award Wild Cards.
- Barbarian Bowl (Arena 3) unlocks Card Evolution with 1 Evo slot, Clan Trading, Duel Decks, Clan Wars and Classic-, Grand- and Mega Draft Challenges. It also allows Lucky Chests to award Evolution Shards.
- Spell Valley (Arena 4) unlocks Card Mastery , allows Lucky Chests to award Battle Banners and Emotes .
- Builder's Workshop (Arena 5) unlocks Heroes with 1 Hero Slot, Star Points and higher tier Pass Royale rewards.
- P.E.K.K.A's Playhouse (Arena 6) unlocks Epic Cards.
- Frozen Peak (Arena 8) unlockes another Tower Troop , besides the Tower Princess .
- Hog Mountain (Arena 10) unlockes the Wild slot (can have evo or hero).
- Electro Valley (Arena 11) unlocks Legendary Cards. It also unlocks the ability to buy Legendary Cards in the Shop for 15,000 Gold .
- Executioner's Kitchen (Arena 16) unlocks Champion Cards.
- Summit of Heroes (Arena 28) unlocks the highest level of the Magic Lucky Chests (16).
- Spirit Square (Arena 32) unlockes Ranked , and is the final normal arena.

### Trophy Road

At various Trophy thresholds, players can accept rewards by tapping the Arena icon above the battling buttons. These rewards come in the form of Cards , Wild Cards , Chests , Gold , Gems , Evolved Barbarians , Evolved Archers, and Emotes . These rewards can only be claimed once. If a player reaches a Trophy threshold for a reward, but drops below the Trophy threshold before claiming it, they will not be able to claim the reward until they reach the required threshold again.

### History

2016

- 7 Arenas were present during Clash Royale's game launch on 4/1/2016, those being Training Camp (Tutorial), Goblin Stadium (Arena 1), Bone Pit (Arena 2), Barbarian Bowl (Arena 3), P.E.K.K.A.'s Playhouse (Arena 4), Spell Valley (Arena 5) and Royal Arena (Arena 6).
- On 2/2/2016, the February 2016 Update introduced Legendary Arena at 3,000 , and removed the battle costs for Battles & Friendly Battles . Previously, the Battle costs for the Arenas were: 1 Gold for the Goblin Stadium, 2 Gold for the Bone Pit, 3 Gold for the Barbarian Bowl, 4 Gold for P.E.K.K.A.'s Playhouse, 5 Gold for Spell Valley and 6 Gold for the Royal Arena.
- On 29/2/2016, the March 2016 Update introduced Builder's Workshop at 1,700 , pushing the lower limit of Royal Arena to 2,000 Trophies. Builder's Workshop became the new Arena 6 while Royal Arena and Legendary Arena became Arenas 7 and 8 respectively.
- On 1/3/2016, a maintenance break fixed Arenas 6 and 7 showing swapped numbers for some players.
- On 3/5/2016, the May 2016 Update increased the donations , requests, and the daily donation limit based on the Arena the player is currently in. It also made it possible for Legendary Cards to appear in the Shop for the players who have reached the tenth Arena. In addition, TV Royale now has replays from every Arena.
- On 4/7/2016, the Tournaments Update ,l introduced Frozen Peak at 2,300 . This would take Legendary Arena's place to become the new Arena 8 while Legendary Arena became Arena 9. It also allowed players to select the Arena where a Friendly Battle would happen.
- On 19/9/2016, the September 2016 Update added Arena Packs in the Shop ; one-time offers that offered Chests , Gold, and Gems for real-world money.
- On 15/12/2016, the December 2016 Update introduced a Jungle Arena at 2,600 , that was later released on 13/1/2017. Legendary Arena became Arena 10, while Jungle Arena took its place as Arena 9.

2017

- On 13/3/2017, the March 2017 Update , renamed the old Legendary Arena to Hog Mountain and made a new Legendary Arena available from 3,800 and above. Despite Legendary Arena moving up in trophies, one could still be able to get Legendary Cards in the Shop from 3,000 . It also changed the sound effect of the Jungle Arena. Previously, the sound effect was identical to that of the Frozen Peak Arena.
- On 9/10/2017, the Epic Quests Update added a new Arena exclusively for battles in the Touchdown mode .
- On 12/12/2017, the Electrifying Update introduced a Electro Valley at 3,400 . Legendary Arena became Arena 12, while Electro Valley took its place as Arena 11.

2018

- On 25/4/2018, the Clan Wars Update added a new Arena exclusively for battles in Clan Wars .
- On 20/6/2018, the Summer 2018 Update changed the Arena at which a Card can be unlocked by having that many Cards in the game. It also made it so that matches above 4,000 would now have a 3 minute overtime.

2019

- On 28/1/2019, the January 2019 Update introduced a Spooky Town at 3,600 , and moved Legendary Arena to 4,000 . As a result, some Cards were moved to different Arenas. It also changed the Trophy requirement for some Arenas.
- On 15/4/2019, the April 2019 Update added the “Trophy Gate”. When the player reaches a new Arena, they cannot drop back down to a previous Arena. However, players can still be demoted to a lower League. The Draft Chests received at the end of each season were also replaced with the Trophy Road, where reaching certain Trophy counts will immediately reward the player with a prize. In addition, getting promoted to a higher Arena or League would reward the player with a Chest boost, where Chest unlock timers are halved for 24 hours.
- Starting from 1/7/2019 with the July 2019 Update , Legendary Arena would now change skins based on Season . In addition, Trophy Road rewards would change depending on the Season.
- On 1/8/2019, a Minor Update fixed an issue for Trophy Road progression indicator sometimes showing the incorrect value.

2020

- On 31/8/2020, the Clan Wars II Update added a new Arena exclusively for battles in Clan Wars , particularly in the Colosseum.

2021

- On 7/6/2021, the Summer 2021 Update added Rascal's Hideout (Season 10 Arena) and Serenity Peak (Season 7 Arena) as Arenas 13 and 14 respectively, at 4,200 and 4,600 respectively, with Legendary Arena being moved to 5,000 . As a result, some cards were moved to different Arenas, making Epic Cards only being able to be unlocked after reaching Arena 2. League requirements were also adjusted, with each League requiring 1,000 more to enter respectively.

2022

- On 26/10/2022, the 2022 Quarter 3 Update added Miner's Mine (Season 34 Arena), Executioner's Kitchen (Season 27 Arena), Royal Crypt (Season 17 Arena), Silent Sanctuary (new to that update), and Dragon Spa (Season 11 Arena), at 5,000, 5,500, 6,000, 6,500, and 7,000 respectively, with Legendary Arena being moved to 7,500 . It also removed the League system from the Trophy Road and moved it to the new Ranked system instead. In addition, P.E.K.K.A.'s Playhouse became the new Arena 6, while Spell Valley and Builder's Workshop became Arenas 4 and 5 respectively. As a result, some cards were moved to different Arenas, with Epic Cards only being able to be unlocked after reaching Arena 6 and Legendary Cards only after reaching Arena 11. Champion Cards would also be unlocked only after reaching Arena 16. Entering a new Arena would now also require reaching a specific King Level .

2023

- On 27/3/2023, the Losers Update added a new Arena exclusively used for Path of Legends.
- On 24/5/2023, a maintenance break changed the King Level requirements for certain Arenas.
- On 19/6/2023, the Card Evolution Update , added Boot Camp (Season 46 Arena), Clash Fest (Season 39 Arena), and PANCAKES! (Season 37 Arena), at 7,500, 8,000, and 8,500 respectively, with Legendary Arena being moved to 9,000 .
- On 13/12/2023, the Tower Troop Update slightly changed the Trophy Road rewards in Arena 13.

2024

- On 10/1/2024, a maintenance break fixed an issue where the Chests Boost effect duration was incorrect.

2025

- On 31/3/2025, the 2025 Quarter 1 update removed the Chest Boost when moving to a new arena
- On 3/6/2025, the 2025 Quarter 2 update changed Trophy Road rewards, while adding Valkalla (Season 55 Arena) at 9,000 , moving Legendary Arena to 9,500 .
- On 7/7/2025, the July 2025 update added Seasonal Arenas from 10,000 to 15,000+ .
- On 24/11/2025, the Level 16 Update moved the first Evolution slot to Arena 3 (from King Level 7) and the second Evolution slot to Arena 10 (from King Level 30). It also made Hero slots available to be unlocked in Arena 15 (first slot) and Arena 24 (second slot).
- On 1/12/2025, the December 2025 Update added Lumberlove (Season 68 Arena), Royal Road (Season 70 Arena), Musketeer Street (Season 77 Arena), and Summit of Heroes (new to that update), at 10,000, 10,500, 11,000, and 11,500 respectively, but temporarily disabled Seasonal Arenas. It also added the Hero Pass Royale for players above Arena 15, allowed Lucky Chests and Hero Boxes to level up based on the player's Arena, and made Magic Items unlockable from Arena 2 onwards.

2026

- On 23/2/2026, the February 2026 Update removed Hero Boxes from the unlockables in every Arena, due to it being removed soon. It also added four Arenas: Magic Academy (Season 59 Arena), Ultimate Clash Pit (Season 63 Arena), Little Prince’s Tavern (Season 53 Arena) and Spirit Square (Season 73 Arena), at 12,000, 12,500, 13,000, and 13,500 respectively.

2027

- On 26/5/2026, The Version History changed some trophy road rewards. It also made it, so that you unlocked many different things through arenas (instead of xp)
