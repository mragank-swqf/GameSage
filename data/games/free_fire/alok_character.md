# Alok Character

Alok is one of the most beginner-friendly characters in Free Fire because his ability directly helps you survive mistakes. His skill focuses on **movement speed and healing over time**, which makes rotating, pushing, and recovering after trades easier than with a pure damage character.

## Active ability: Drop the Beat

When activated, Alok creates a music aura that:

- **Increases movement speed** for you and nearby teammates inside the aura
- **Restores HP over time** while the effect is active

Think of it as a short window where your squad can reposition or re-engage with extra sustain. New players often die while healing with med kits in the open — Alok's regen lets you slide behind cover and recover while still moving.

### Practical tips for new players

- Activate **before** pushing a compound, not after you are already one-shot.
- The speed boost helps you cross open ground during zone rotations — use it when the safe area is far away.
- Stay near teammates so they get the heal aura; Alok is a support pick, not a solo frag character.

## Passive synergy

Pair Alok with loadouts that reward aggressive repositioning:

- **SMG + shotgun** — Speed into close fights, heal through chip damage, back out if the trade goes wrong.
- **Stable AR (SCAR / M4A1)** — Speed lets you take better angles at mid range without exposing yourself as long.

Avoid camping in one spot for an entire circle — Alok's value is in **moving fights**, not holding a window for five minutes.

## Best weapon pairings

| Primary | Secondary | Why |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| MP40 / UMP | M1014 | Speed into rooms, shotgun finish, heal while repositioning |
| SCAR | M1887 | Balanced mid-range with panic CQC option |
| M4A1 | P90 or second SMG | Forgiving AR for new aim, SMG for final zone |

## Squad role

In a four-player squad, Alok is the **entry support**:

- Go first into unknown areas with speed boost active so teammates can trade if you get knocked.
- Call out when your ability is ready — coordinated pushes with Alok win more than random rushes.
- Carry extra med kits anyway; ability healing supplements kits, it does not replace them.

## When to pick Alok vs other characters

**Pick Alok when:**

- You are new and die during rotations or heal animations
- Your squad plays fast, close-range styles
- You need forgiveness while learning map timing

**Consider another character when:**

- Your team already has strong heal/support and lacks raw damage
- You want long-range sniper angles (Alok does not help holding 80m peeks)

## Common beginner mistakes

- Popping the ability in a fair 1v1 mid-fight without cover — you still lose to good aim.
- Running too far ahead of the squad because of speed boost — you get isolated and focused.
- Forgetting teammates need to be in aura range — communicate before you activate.

Alok teaches good habits: **move with purpose, fight near cover, reset after trades**. Master those even if you switch characters later.
