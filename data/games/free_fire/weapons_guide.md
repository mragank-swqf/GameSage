# Weapons Guide

Free Fire matches are short and fast, so weapon choice matters from the first loot rotation. You rarely have time to fully upgrade a gun, which means your base pick — AR, SMG, shotgun, sniper, or marksman — should match how your squad wants to take fights.

## Weapon classes and when to use them

### Assault rifles (mid-range backbone)

Reliable options include **AK**, **SCAR**, **M4A1**, **Groza**, and **FAMAS**. ARs win most fair fights between 30–80 meters if you burst-fire instead of full-auto spraying.

- **AK / Groza** — High damage, harder recoil. Pair with a foregrip character or tap-fire at range.
- **M4A1 / SCAR** — Stable, forgiving. Best default for ranked when you need consistent 2–3 shot trades.
- **FAMAS** — Strong burst at close-mid range; fall off at long distance.

### SMGs (close-range entry)

**MP40**, **UMP**, **Vector**, and **VSS** dominate inside compounds and final-zone buildings.

- Push with SMG when the circle forces close fights or your team plays aggressive rush.
- Weak beyond ~40 meters — always carry a secondary or plan to swap loot after first knock.

### Shotguns (one-shot potential)

**M1014**, **M1887**, and **MAG-7** end fights instantly under 10 meters.

- One shotgun player per squad is enough; the rest should hold AR/SMG for follow-up.
- Peek corners pre-aimed — shotgun fights are decided in the first shot.

### Snipers and marksmen (range control)

**AWM**, **M82B**, **Kar98k**, and **SVD** punish exposed enemies.

- AWM one-shots head with high skill expression; hold angles on open terrain maps.
- SVD and SKS work as DMR picks when your team lacks long-range pressure but you do not want full sniper scope swap speed.

## Sample ranked loadouts

### Balanced squad AR (solo queue safe)

- Primary: **SCAR** or **M4A1**
- Secondary: **M1014** or **MP40** for house clearing
- Playstyle: Hold power positions at mid range, third-party only when you have cover to disengage

### Rush composition

- Primary: **UMP** or **Vector**
- Secondary: **M1887** for instant finish inside rooms
- Playstyle: Drive toward zone edge fights early, collapse on knocked enemies before reset heals

### Long-range control

- Primary: **AK** or **Groza** for mid
- Secondary: **AWM** or **Kar98k**
- Playstyle: One player holds overwatch while others loot safely; rotate early to high ground

## Recoil and fight discipline

- Drag your fire downward in short bursts on AK and Groza — do not hold trigger at 60+ meters.
- Pre-aim head level at every corner; TTK is low so first shot advantage wins most duels.
- Swap to your secondary during reload or when an enemy pushes inside shotgun range.

## Loot priority in first 30 seconds

Land on a zone with guaranteed weapon spawns. Pick one AR or SMG you can control, one shotgun or second close option, med kits, and level 2 vest if available. Extended fights are rare — armor and healing often matter more than a fully modded gun.
